Hi everyone,

may1937, ramana and I just had a meeting on IRC. We talked about what we
need to do, and what we should focus on. We decided that the best first
step is to create a working simulator. This will help all of us understand
more about the architecture. 

We want to start from anew (not using gdspsim), and there are three
options we can pursue: cgen, igen, or from scratch. igen and cgen are
simulator generators, and cgen even generates binutils and some other
stuff. We haven't decided which we want to pursue yet, but cgen looks the
snazziest.

We will have logs of the previous meeting on the website,
http://gcc-c54x.berlios.de, as soon as we learn enough html to get it up
there. ;) The next meeting is tentatively scheduled for Tuesday, 18 Oct,
15:00 GMT (8am California, 4pm Austria, 8:30pm India). If that doesn't work
for someone, let me know.

Talk to you later,

-Bryan
